    Professional Liability for Community Banks

Protx Risk Management LLC was founded by experienced insurance professionals who understand Community Banking to provide the best products, coverage and service to insurance agents representing America's Community Banks.

OUR TEAM

  • ROBERT L. HITCH, CPCU, ARe. Chairman

    Bob has over 40 years experience in the insurance industry in underwriting and management roles, serving as Divisional President at Great American Insurance Company, and President and CEO of Mitsui Marine Management USA. As a consultant he has advised major international insurance organizations on business development, underwriting, and claims. He holds a BS from the University of Kentucky.

  • MICHAEL WHITBECK, President

    Before joining Protx Risk Management LLC, Michael specialized in professional lines for CNA where he served as Underwriting Director, responsible for the East Coast managing a multi-million dollar book of Community Bank business and multiple underwriters. Prior to that Michael worked for AIG. He has over 10 years experience underwriting professional lines for community banks. He has a BS from Northeastern University where he majored in Finance and Insurance.

  • EDWARD MONGON, Vice President

    Edward also specializes in underwriting professional liability for Community Banks, where he served until recently as Senior Underwriter at AIG. He has over 7 years of experience in the sector. Additionally, he has an MBA from Rutgers University in Finance and Management and has earned the designation of RPLU.

  • MARK VITTORIA, Senior Underwriter

    Mark has over four years of insurance experience, having specialized in underwriting professional lines for American International Group, Inc. (AIG) before joining Protx Risk Management, LLC. Prior to AIG, Mark worked more than nine years as a senior accountant and auditor. He is a graduate of Roger Williams University where he majored in accounting and minored in psychology and computer information systems.
